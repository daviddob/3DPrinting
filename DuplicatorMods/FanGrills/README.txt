# Summary

This is a set of four honeycomb fan grilles for cooling fans on your electronic project, 3d printers or wherever else you can use them. There are sizes  to fit 40, 50, 60 and 80mm square cooling fans. The honeycomb is recessed 1mm on the fan side to ensure clearance to the blades.
